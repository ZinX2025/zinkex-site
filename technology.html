Technology & Architecture

Zinkex is modular and scalable. The core components are:

ZINX Token (ERC‑20)

Utility & governance token used for staking, voting and fees — fully compatible with Ethereum wallets and exchanges.

Staking Contract

Handles deposits, withdrawals and reward distribution with transparent on‑chain accounting.

Governance / AI Adapter

Bridges off‑chain AI suggestions to on‑chain proposals. All changes require multisig approval and a timelock.

Off‑chain AI Module

Ingests blockchain metrics and external signals to produce explainable recommendations — never self‑executing.

Security

  • Multisig for critical actions
  • Timelock before upgrades take effect
  • Emergency pause (Pausable)
  • Independent audits & continuous monitoring

Compliance

  • MiCA‑oriented token design
  • GDPR data‑minimization principles
  • NIS2‑inspired operational security
